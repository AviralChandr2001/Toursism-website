featured places

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The Golden temple is located in the holy city of a Amritsar. The Golden temple is famous for its full golden dome, most sacred pilgrim spots for Sikhs

Gwalior Fort, Madhya Pradesh

The Gwalior Fort commonly known as the Gwalior Qila, is a hill fort near Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, India.

Hampi, Karnataka

Hampi is an ancient village in the state of the Karnataka.

Mehrangarh Fort, Rajasthan

The fort was named Mehrangarh, meaning 'fort of the sun' reference to the clan's mythical descent from the sun god Surya

Camel Safari, Rajasthan

There are several places in Rajasthan where you can go for Camel Safari, and the best include - Jaisalmer, Bikaner, Pushkar, and Jodhpur.

Alappuzha, Kerela

Alappuzha, famous for its boat races, beaches, marine products and coir industry, is a world renowned backwater tourist destination of India.

Umngot river, Meghalaya

The Umngot River, also known as the Dawki River and Wah Umngot, is a river which flows through Dawki, located at the bottom of the West Jaintia Hills district in the Indian state of Meghalaya.

Mon, Myanmar

Mon is home to several tribes. Mon is famous for raw beauty of nature, the tourist enjoy the destination far away from the city chaos.

Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Tamil Nadu

Vivekananda Rock Memorial is a monument and popular tourist attraction in Kanyakumari, India's southernmost tip.
